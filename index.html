Biography

I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2019. I currently work as Research Assistant in ESQuaReD (E2) Laboratory. Before joining E2, I worked as Research Assistant in Systems Biology and Biomedical Informatics (SBBI) Laboratory. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from University Fumec (Belo Horizonte, Brazil) in 2015.

I am a full stack software engineer that loves to work with new and innovative technologies. I am excited to transform incredible ideas into reliable, scalable and easy to use software to help businesses to make a dent in the universe.

I have experience in developing solutions from zero to hero (RESTful APIs, web services, database design, machine learning models) with Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, Python, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, AngularJS, Node.js, Express, C++, Android, scikit-learn, Pandas, SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, SQL Server, Git and AWS
