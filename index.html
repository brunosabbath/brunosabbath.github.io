Biography

Bruno obtained his M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2019. He currently works as Research Assistant in ESQuaReD (E2) Laboratory. Before joining E2, worked as Research Assistant in Systems Biology and Biomedical Informatics (SBBI) Laboratory.. He received his B.S. in Computer Science from Fumec University (Belo Horizonte, Brazil) in 2015.

His primary research interests are in software engineering, data analysis, machine learning and computer vision. His research focuses static analysis approaches to detect incompatibility issues in Android apps.
